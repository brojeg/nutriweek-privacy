What to include

To help us fix things quickly, please mention:

  • What you were trying to do and what happened.
  • Your device model and Android version.
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if you can.
  • Whether it happens every time or just once.

Or just write to nutriweek.support@gmail.com — every report helps make NutriWeek better.
