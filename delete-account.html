Delete Your NutriWeek Account & Data

App: NutriWeek · Last updated: 17 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your NutriWeek account and the data associated with it. NutriWeek is a weekly meal-planning app operated by the NutriWeek developer.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open NutriWeek and make sure you are signed in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Tap “Delete account & data”.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. Your account and data are then permanently deleted from our servers.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email nutriweek.support@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject “Delete my account”. We will verify your identity and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes the personal data associated with it, including:

  • Your account identity (name, email, and user ID from Google Sign-In).
  • Your app content: dishes, weekly menus and meal plans, shopping lists, and dietary preferences.
  • Nutrition information you generated for planned meals.
  • Your household membership and any premium/billing status record we hold.
  • Your usage and quota records.

Nutrition data you previously exported to Android Health Connect is stored by Health Connect, not by NutriWeek; remove it in the Health Connect app if you wish.

What may be retained, and for how long

DataRetention
Account and app content (above) Deleted from active systems immediately on request; purged from encrypted backups within 30 days.
Transaction / purchase records Subscription and payment records are held by Google Play and may be retained by us only as required for tax, accounting, or legal obligations.
Minimal security/diagnostic logs Anonymized or short-lived operational logs retained only as needed for security and reliability.
