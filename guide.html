NutriWeek — How It Works

User guide · Last updated 17 June 2026

NutriWeek is a weekly meal-planning app for households. Plan a whole week of meals, turn that plan into an organized shopping list, keep a personal dish library, track nutrition, and (optionally) share the plan with your family — with AI helpers along the way. This guide explains every feature and how to use it.

Getting started

NutriWeek uses a simple bottom navigation with four sections — Menu, Dishes, Shopping, and Nutrition — plus Settings in the side menu.

  • Sign in with your Google account to create your NutriWeek account and sync your data across devices.
  • On first launch, a short onboarding helps you set up; you can set global dietary preferences that the app respects everywhere.
  • The app comes pre-filled with a starter set of dishes and categories in your language, so you can start planning right away.

Weekly menu

The Menu tab is where you plan the week. Dishes are organized by category (e.g. Breakfast, Lunch/Dinner, Soups, Sides, Snacks).

  • Pick dishes for the week from your library; selected dishes form that week's menu.
  • Navigate weeks with the arrows to plan ahead or review past weeks.
  • Two views: a simple weekly list, or a day-by-day plan (toggle in Settings).
  • AI menu analysis reviews your week's balance and suggests improvements or new dishes to round it out.

Dishes & recipes

The Dishes tab is your personal recipe library, filtered by category.

  • Add your own dishes with ingredients, and edit or remove any dish.
  • AI recipe ideas generate new dish suggestions tailored to your preferences and categories.
  • Generate a full recipe for any dish, and suggest ingredients when you're adding one.
  • Dish photos are generated automatically so your library looks great.
Per-category rules: you can give each category its own dietary guidance (e.g. "kid-friendly" for snacks), and the AI follows it when suggesting dishes.

Shopping list

The Shopping tab turns your weekly menu into an organized list.

  • Generate the weekly list from your selected meals, scaled to your family size (adults & children).
  • Items are grouped by aisle/category so nothing gets missed, with a progress bar as you shop.
  • Mark items bought or not-needed, and add your own items manually.
  • "What to cook from the cart" suggests dishes you can make from what you've already bought.
  • Once you've shopped, NutriWeek can highlight which planned meals are ready to cook based on what you have.
  • Share the list with others.

Nutrition & Health Connect (Premium)

  • NutriWeek estimates per-serving nutrition (calories and macronutrients) for the dishes in your week.
  • The Nutrition dashboard shows your weekly total and daily average calories and macros.
  • Export to Health Connect: with your permission, NutriWeek can write the nutrition of your planned meals to Android Health Connect. It only writes what you choose to export and never reads your existing health data.

Family sharing (Premium)

  • Create a household and invite family members with a join code.
  • Your weekly menu, day plan, and shopping list are shared across the household and sync instantly, so everyone sees the same plan.
  • Your personal dish library and preferences stay your own.
  • Members can join or leave a household at any time.

AI helpers

AI features make planning faster. They include:

  • Generating balanced weekly menus and individual dish ideas.
  • Writing recipes and suggesting ingredients.
  • Creating dish photos.
  • Analyzing your week and recommending improvements.
  • Building your shopping list and suggesting meals from your cart.

AI suggestions follow your stated dietary preferences and the app's language.

Reminders

With your permission, NutriWeek can send a gentle weekly notification to remind you to plan next week's menu. You can turn notifications off anytime in your device settings.

Your data & sync

  • NutriWeek is offline-first: your plan is always available on your device, and changes sync to the cloud when you're online so you can switch devices.
  • Your account and content are tied to your Google sign-in.
  • We don't sell your data or use it for advertising. For full details, see the Privacy Policy.

Settings & account

  • Language — choose the app language (independent of your phone language).
  • Menu view — switch between weekly list and day-by-day plan.
  • Dietary preferences — global rules the AI respects.
  • Sign out.
  • Delete account & data — permanently removes your account and content. See the deletion guide.

Languages & offline

NutriWeek is available in English, Russian, French, Spanish, and German, and works offline for everything except AI features and cloud sync, which need a connection.
