NutriWeek Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the NutriWeek mobile application ("NutriWeek", "the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using NutriWeek you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

NutriWeek is a weekly meal-planning and nutrition app for households. The app is operated by the NutriWeek developer ("we"). For any privacy question or request, contact us at: brojeg@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

CategoryExamplesWhy
Account information Email address, display name, and account identifier provided when you sign in with Google. To create and secure your account and sync your data across your devices.
App content you create Meal plans, weekly menus, dish library, shopping lists, dietary preferences, household membership, and nutrition information for planned meals. To provide the core planning features and (optionally) share a plan within your household.

3. AI-generated suggestions

When you use NutriWeek's AI features (for example, generating a weekly menu or dish ideas), the inputs needed to produce a suggestion — such as your stated dietary preferences and meal context — are sent to Google's Gemini API through our secure backend to generate a response. These inputs are used only to produce your result and are not used to identify you. Do not enter information you do not wish to be processed by an AI service.

4. Health Connect (nutrition export)

If you are a premium user and choose to enable it, NutriWeek can write the nutrition information of your planned meals (such as calories and macronutrients) to Android Health Connect, using the WRITE_NUTRITION permission.

  • NutriWeek only writes nutrition records you choose to export. It does not read any of your existing Health Connect or health data.
  • This feature is off until you explicitly grant the Health Connect permission, and you can revoke it at any time in Health Connect or Android settings.
  • Data written to Health Connect is governed by Health Connect's own controls. We do not sell, and do not use for advertising, any health or nutrition data. Health data is never transferred to third parties except as required to provide the feature you requested.

5. Notifications

With your permission, NutriWeek sends local reminder notifications (for example, meal-planning reminders). You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app's features.
  • To sync and back up your data across your devices and household.
  • To unlock and validate premium functionality.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability and security.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your content or health data for advertising.

7. Service providers

We rely on Google services to operate the app, which process data on our behalf:

  • Firebase Authentication — sign-in and account management.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores and syncs your app content.
  • Cloud Functions & App Check — secure backend processing and abuse prevention.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Google Analytics for Firebase — crash and usage diagnostics.
  • Google Gemini API — AI generation, as described in Section 3.
  • Google Play Billing — purchase processing.

These providers process data under Google's terms and privacy policy (policies.google.com/privacy).

8. Data retention

We keep your account and app content for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your personal content stored in our backend is deleted. Diagnostic data is retained only for the period needed to keep the service reliable and secure.

9. Your choices and rights

  • Access & deletion: you may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting brojeg@gmail.com, or by using the in-app account-deletion option where available.
  • Permissions: you can revoke notification and Health Connect permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA; contact us to exercise them.

10. Children

NutriWeek is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

11. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted transport and app-integrity attestation, to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice.

13. Contact

Questions or requests: brojeg@gmail.com.
